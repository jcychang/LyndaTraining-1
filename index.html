UMD students, faculty, and staff have unlimited access to lynda.com, an online library of instructional videos covering the latest software, creative, and business skills.

Sign In

With thousands of courses taught by industry experts — and more items added often — lynda.com is available whenever you're ready to learn. You can even view it on your iPhone, iPad, Android phone or tablet, or other mobile device. Use it to develop skills in Adobe Creative Suite, Microsoft Office, leadership, project management, social media, video production, and a wide range of other topics.
